## 7. Faculty of Transport Systems and Mechanical Engineering

**Source:** https://gtu.ge/stmm/en/about/structure/faculty-council.php

### Faculty Board Structure
- The faculty board includes all academic staff members and student self-government representatives.
- Student self-government representatives constitute 1/3 of the board.
- The board is authorized if more than half of the members are present.
- Decisions are made by majority vote.
- In the dean's absence, at least 1/3 of board members or the deputy dean can convene the board.

### Board Authorities
- Develops and submits the faculty budget
- Elects the dean by secret ballot
- Develops strategic plans and training/research programs
- Develops faculty structure and regulations
- Develops Dissertation Board regulations
- Elects the head of quality assurance service
- Creates a permanent ethics commission
- Conducts disciplinary proceedings against students
- Forms permanent and temporary commissions
- Can consider early termination of dean's authority
- Elects members of the academic board and senate
